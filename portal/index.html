⚕ PACS Demo Platform

Clinical integration stack — DICOM imaging, AI pipelines, EHR, LIS, RIS and FHIR R4 interoperability, all running locally via Docker Compose.

🖼️
OHIF Viewer
/ohif/
Web-based DICOM viewer. Browse studies, view series, and inspect AI secondary-capture overlays and segmentation masks.
Imaging
🗄️
Orthanc PACS
/orthanc/
DICOM server (PACS). Stores all imaging studies. Exposes DICOMweb REST API consumed by OHIF and the AI Controller.
Imaging · DICOM
📋
RIS
/ris/
Radiology Information System. Manages imaging orders, accession numbers and radiology reports (Draft → Preliminary → Final).
Clinical
🤖
AI Controller
/ai-controller/
Manages AI analysis pipelines (POC X-Ray, POC CT). Configures auto-trigger rules by modality and saves results back to PACS.
AI · Pipelines
🏥
EHR
/ehr/
Electronic Health Records. Patient registration, encounter management, clinical orders, scheduling and CDSS alert evaluation.
Clinical
🧪
LIS
/lis/
Laboratory Information System. Specimen accessioning, instrument management and result validation with Westgard QC rules.
Clinical · Lab
⚡
DICOM Simulator
/simulator/
Generates synthetic DICOM series (CR, DX, CT, MR, US…) and pushes them directly to Orthanc to seed the demo with test data.
Dev Tool
🔗
FHIR Bridge
/fhir-bridge/
Translates internal clinical events into FHIR R4 resources (Patient, ImagingStudy, ServiceRequest, DiagnosticReport) and pushes to HAPI.
FHIR R4
PACS Demo — local Docker Compose stack — all data is synthetic
