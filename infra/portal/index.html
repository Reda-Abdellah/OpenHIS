👤

⚕ OpenHIS

Clinical integration stack — OpenMRS O3 EMR, OpenELIS LIS, Odoo ERP, DICOM imaging, AI pipelines, RIS, HL7 and FHIR R4 interoperability, all running locally via Docker Compose.

Clinical
🏥
OpenMRS O3
/openmrs/spa/
Electronic Medical Records (O3 React SPA). Patient registration, encounters, clinical orders, FHIR R4 REST and FHIR2 APIs.
Clinical · FHIR R4
🧪
OpenELIS Global 2
/OpenELIS-Global/
Laboratory Information System. Specimen accessioning, result entry and validation. Exposes FHIR R4 DiagnosticReport and ServiceRequest resources.
Clinical · Lab · FHIR R4
📋
RIS
/ris/
Radiology Information System. Manages imaging orders, accession numbers and radiology reports (Draft → Preliminary → Final). Syncs orders from OpenMRS.
Clinical · Radiology
🙋
Patient Portal
/patient-portal/
Patient self-service access. View lab results, imaging reports, diagnoses, allergies, appointments and billing summary.
Patient-Facing
Finance & Administration
💼
Odoo 17
/odoo/
Hospital ERP. Patient billing and invoicing, pharmacy stock control, supplier management, financial reporting and HR administration.
Finance · ERP
Imaging
🖼️
OHIF Viewer
/ohif/
Web-based DICOM viewer. Browse studies, view series, and inspect AI secondary-capture overlays and segmentation masks.
Imaging
🗄️
Orthanc PACS
/orthanc/
DICOM server (PACS). Stores all imaging studies. Exposes DICOMweb REST API consumed by OHIF and the AI Controller.
Imaging · DICOM
⚡
DICOM Simulator
/simulator/
Generates synthetic DICOM series (CR, DX, CT, MR, US…) and pushes them directly to Orthanc to seed the demo with test data.
Dev Tool
AI & Pipelines
🤖
AI Controller
/ai-controller/
Manages AI analysis pipelines (POC X-Ray, POC CT). Configures auto-trigger rules by modality and saves results back to PACS.
AI · Pipelines
Interoperability
🔗
Integration Hub
/integration-hub/
Bidirectional FHIR sync engine. Polls OpenMRS and OpenELIS, routes lab orders and results, pushes DiagnosticReports, and logs all sync events.
FHIR R4 · Sync
📡
HL7 Gateway
/hl7/
HL7 v2 message builder and MLLP gateway. Send and receive ADT, ORM and ORU messages with a full inbound message history browser.
HL7 v2 · MLLP
🪪
MPI
/mpi/
Master Patient Index. Cross-reference registry linking patient identities across OpenMRS, OpenELIS and Odoo by MRN. Probabilistic deduplication.
Identity · FHIR
Infrastructure
📊
Analytics
/analytics/
Platform-wide metrics and reporting. Scheduled data collection from OpenMRS, OpenELIS and RIS, aggregate KPIs and CSV export.
Metrics · Export
🛠️
Admin Dashboard
/admin/
Platform management plane. Service registry and health status, profile enable/disable, audit log, system announcements and live event stream.
Admin · Registry
🔑
Keycloak Admin
/keycloak/admin/
Identity and access management. Create users, assign roles (clinician, lab-tech, radiologist, pharmacist, patient), manage SSO clients and sessions. Log in with Keycloak master admin credentials.
IAM · SSO
