⚕ OpenHIS

Clinical integration stack — DICOM imaging, AI pipelines, EHR, LIS, RIS, Pharmacy, MPI, HL7 and FHIR R4 interoperability, all running locally via Docker Compose.

Clinical
🏥
EHR
/ehr/
Electronic Health Records. Patient registration, encounter management, clinical orders, scheduling and CDSS alert evaluation.
Clinical
🧪
LIS
/lis/
Laboratory Information System. Specimen accessioning, instrument management and result validation with Westgard QC rules.
Clinical · Lab
📋
RIS
/ris/
Radiology Information System. Manages imaging orders, accession numbers and radiology reports (Draft → Preliminary → Final).
Clinical · Radiology
💊
Pharmacy
/pharmacy/
Medication management. Drug catalog, prescription workflow, dispensing records, stock control and Medication Administration Record (MAR).
Clinical · Pharmacy
🪪
MPI
/mpi/
Master Patient Index. Probabilistic identity matching, demographic deduplication and cross-referencing across all clinical systems.
Clinical · Identity
🙋
Patient Portal
/patient-portal/
Patient self-service access. View personal encounters, clinical documents, lab results and upcoming appointments.
Patient-Facing
Imaging
🖼️
OHIF Viewer
/ohif/
Web-based DICOM viewer. Browse studies, view series, and inspect AI secondary-capture overlays and segmentation masks.
Imaging
🗄️
Orthanc PACS
/orthanc/
DICOM server (PACS). Stores all imaging studies. Exposes DICOMweb REST API consumed by OHIF and the AI Controller.
Imaging · DICOM
⚡
DICOM Simulator
/simulator/
Generates synthetic DICOM series (CR, DX, CT, MR, US…) and pushes them directly to Orthanc to seed the demo with test data.
Dev Tool
AI & Pipelines
🤖
AI Controller
/ai-controller/
Manages AI analysis pipelines (POC X-Ray, POC CT). Configures auto-trigger rules by modality and saves results back to PACS.
AI · Pipelines
Interoperability
🔗
FHIR Bridge
/fhir-bridge/
Translates internal clinical events into FHIR R4 resources (Patient, ImagingStudy, ServiceRequest, DiagnosticReport) and pushes to HAPI.
FHIR R4
📡
HL7 Gateway
/hl7/
HL7 v2 message builder and MLLP gateway. Send and receive ADT, ORM and ORU messages with a full inbound message history browser.
HL7 v2 · MLLP
Infrastructure & Admin
📊
Analytics
/analytics/
Platform-wide metrics and reporting. Scheduled data collection, aggregate KPIs across all modules and CSV export.
Metrics · Export
⚙️
Admin Dashboard
/admin/
Staff operations panel. Real-time health monitoring of all services, admin user management, system config key-value store, broadcast announcements and full audit log.
